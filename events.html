Saturday, December 7, 2019

MIS-Comp Sci Projects Competition

9:00am - 9:00pm
E3-270 EITC Building

In other faculties, this event is generally known as Hackathon. For us at Asper, MIS-Comp Sci Projects Competition is more appropriate in describing the event and its value to MISA members.

MISA members will get the opportunity to apply their knowledge and skills to come up with solutions to real-world problems through critical thinking in a group setting. Express your creativity and innovation while widening your knowledge and further enhancing your skills.

This event is open to all students at the University of Manitoba! No qualifications or experience is required! If interested, please refer to the Local Hack Day Website.
