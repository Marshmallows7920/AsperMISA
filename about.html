Who is MISA?

Founded in 1998, Management Information Systems Association (MISA) is a non-profit organization operated by only students. We are recognized by the Commerce Students Association (CSA) within the I.H. Asper School of Business at the University of Manitoba.

Since 2001, we have hosted an annual networking event that connects students with working professionals in the business technology field called BizTek.

MISA’s main demographic are the Management Information Systems (MIS) students. However, ALL individuals inside and outside the Asper School of Business are welcome to join the group & attend our events! Membership is FREE for everybody!

Why do we exist?

Our mission is to provide a service regarding anything related to MIS to the University of Manitoba community and by extension, the local business community in Manitoba.

What do we do?

We deliver this service through 3 key initiatives:
1) Providing opportunities by bringing you job postings, company information sessions, networking events, and the like!
2) Providing resources by hosting speaker sessions with working professionals, materials regarding the MIS major, and the like!
3) Providing a community dedicated towards MIS by hosting an online forum on Slack, hosting an offline forum through our events, and the like!

When and where are you guys in operation?

We generally operate during the University of Manitoba academic year from the beginning of September to the end of April. Around March/April is when a new team of executives are elected in to operate for the next academic year!

Our main base of operations are at the I.H. Asper School of Business at the University of Manitoba.
Address: 181 Freedman Crescent, Winnipeg, Manitoba R3T, CA
